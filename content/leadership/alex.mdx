With decades of experience in the communication industry and Systems Engineering post-graduate work from Georgetown University, Alex is an accomplished professional in building and operating telecommunication networks across Asia Pacific. Before joining Samespace, he led one of the main VoIP service providers in the Philippines, spearheading the technology shift in the contact center and BPO space from TDM to SIP Trunking to Managed PBX platforms.

As the Senior VP Sales Philippines, he oversees Samespace’s strategic partnerships and business development efforts in the Philippines and Asia.
